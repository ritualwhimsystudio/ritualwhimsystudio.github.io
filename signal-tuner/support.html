FAQ

How does Signal Tuner work?

When an urge hits, open the app and tap Tune. Move your finger across the screen to lock into a signal — guided by sound, color, and haptics — until your attention stabilizes. Then choose a quick reset: Breathe, Body Reset, or Focus Tap. Each action takes under two minutes.

Does Signal Tuner work offline?

Yes, fully. All features work without an internet connection.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Signal Tuner requires no account and no sign-in.

Does Signal Tuner collect my data?

No. All data stays on your device. We don't collect analytics, usage data, or personal information. See our privacy policy for details.

What are the reset options?

Breathe is a short guided breathing cycle to calm your nervous system. Body Reset is a 60-second physical reset (wall sit or plank). Focus Tap is rapid tapping to anchor your attention.

Is Signal Tuner free?

Yes. The full core experience is free with no gating. Optional audio packs and visual themes are available as one-time purchases.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes everything.
