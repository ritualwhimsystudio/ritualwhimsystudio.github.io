Privacy Policy

Signal Tuner: Urge Reset · Effective date: March 16, 2026

Overview

Signal Tuner is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information stays on your device.

Data Collection

Signal Tuner does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Cookies or tracking pixels

Local Storage

The app stores a small amount of data locally on your device to support its functionality:

  • Your redirect activity counts and timestamps (used to display your personal stats)
  • A flag indicating whether you have completed onboarding
  • Your settings preferences

This data is stored using on-device storage only. It is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

Third-Party Services

Signal Tuner does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app does not make any network requests.

Children's Privacy

Signal Tuner does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
