Privacy Policy

Signal Tuner: Urge Reset · Last updated March 2026

Overview

Signal Tuner: Urge Reset ("the App") is developed by Alyssa Wei ("we," "us," or "our"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Collection

Signal Tuner is designed to be private by default. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers

No Account Required

The App does not require an account, sign-in, or any form of registration. There is no cloud sync or server-side storage of your data.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Crash Reporting

The App may collect anonymous crash reports to help us identify and fix bugs. Crash reports do not contain personally identifiable information.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all data. There is no server-side data to delete.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. No data is stored externally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
