About Signal Tuner

When an urge hits, you don't need more willpower. You need something to do.

Signal Tuner helps you interrupt the moment, reset, and move on. Instead of trying to "power through," you redirect the energy into something concrete, simple, and effective.

Open the app and tap Tune. Move your finger across the screen to lock into a signal — guided by sound, color, and haptics — until your attention stabilizes. Then choose a quick reset: Breathe, Body Reset, or Focus Tap. Each action takes under two minutes.

What makes it different

  • Most apps in this space track behavior, block content, or count streaks — Signal Tuner gives you something to do in the moment
  • Fast — designed for low-energy, high-friction situations
  • Physical and sensory — sound, color, haptics, and movement
  • Interruptive — breaks the loop instead of analyzing it
  • No thinking required

Reset options

  • Breathe — a short guided breathing cycle to calm your nervous system
  • Body Reset — a 60-second physical reset (wall sit or plank)
  • Focus Tap — rapid tapping to anchor your attention

Designed for real moments

  • Works fully offline
  • No account required
  • No tracking or data collection
  • Built for low-energy, high-friction situations
