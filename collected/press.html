About Collected

Collected is a modern sticker chart app designed for real life — for adults and families. It turns habit tracking into something visual, tactile, and rewarding. Not streak-based pressure. Not overwhelming dashboards. Just small progress you can see.

Place beautiful stickers on custom charts, watch your collection grow, and unlock rewards that actually matter to you. No pressure, no shame.

What makes it different

  • It’s collectible — instead of checking boxes, you place stickers and build something
  • It’s not shame-based — no streak panic, no red Xs, progress accumulates and doesn’t reset
  • It’s intentionally designed — beautiful themes, thoughtful visuals, feels like a keepsake not a spreadsheet
  • 100+ collectible stickers across themed packs with new drops monthly
  • Works fully offline, private by default
