FAQ

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you've enabled cloud backup (premium), your data syncs through your Apple ID.

Can I delete a chart?

Yes. Open the chart, tap the edit icon, and delete it from there.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Collected uses a one-time purchase, not a subscription. There's nothing to cancel.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete individual charts from the chart's edit menu. You can also delete all local data from Settings.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. If you have a premium account with cloud sync, go to Settings → Account & Sync and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data from our servers. If you're on the free tier, all data is local â€” deleting the app removes everything.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Tap the "Restore Purchase" button in Settings.
