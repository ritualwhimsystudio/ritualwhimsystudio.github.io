About

Small, beautiful tools for becoming.

We believe habits shouldn't feel like punishment. Progress shouldn't feel invisible. And identity shouldn't feel accidental.

Ritual Whimsy Studio builds apps that make everyday growth feel intentional — even magical.

Origin

I've always been drawn to systems — journals, charts, trackers, frameworks. But most productivity tools felt sterile, overwhelming, or built around guilt.

I wanted something different. Something playful and visual. Something that helped you see who you're becoming.

This studio was born from the idea that small actions, repeated with intention, shape identity. And if we're going to build better habits, we deserve tools that feel beautiful.

Who we design for

  • Visual thinkers
  • Neurodivergent brains
  • People who crave progress but resist rigid productivity culture
  • Parents building tiny rituals with their kids
  • Anyone who wants growth to feel alive

Ritual over routine.

Collection over checkboxes.

Identity over optimization.

We don't build tools to make you “more productive.” We build tools to help you become more yourself.

What's next

Collected is our first app. The long-term vision is a suite of small, intentional tools that help people build habits, track growth, and shape identity — not through pressure, but through ritual.
